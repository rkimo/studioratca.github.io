Emily Allan is a designer and artist based in Toronto, ON. Her work is graphic, illustration and installation oriented with  an emphasized interest on small space-living. Currently Emily is exploring the integration of her illustration practices with three-dimensional mediums. CV / email
Dominique Di Libero is a hands-on designer that is interested in how the practice of design can be pushed forward by exploring forms of making and craft. Her work focuses on 3D objects and installations. CV /email
Dominique and Emily are both fourth year students studying Interior Design at Ryerson University and together they make up the freelance design collective studiorat. studiorat is a growing practice that is focused on installation work and experimental design fabrication practices, looking to establish research and dialogue within the city of Toronto. Presently, we are exploring how fabricated pneumatic environments can create common space within a variety of contexts.
