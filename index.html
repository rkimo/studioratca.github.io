studio rat
studio rat is a growing creative practice that is focused on installation work and experimental design fabrication looking to establish research and dialogue within the city of Toronto. Presently, we are exploring how fabricated pneumatic environments can create common space within a variety of contexts.
series 00 is our first pneumatic prototype. This collection of images is shot outside of the cityscape in Georgian Bay, Ontario.
studiorat's recent bts photo series explores the spacial qualities of their first of four inflatable prototypes with Toronto pop duo moneyphone
currently studiorat is working in partnership with DesignTO to design and fabricate the city's annual design week opening night installation
