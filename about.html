Dominique and Emily are both fourth year students studying Interior Design at Ryerson University and together they make up the freelance design collective studiorat. studiorat is a growing practice that is focused on installation work and experimental design fabrication practices, looking to establish research and dialogue within the city of Toronto. Presently, we are exploring how fabricated pneumatic environments can create common space within a variety of contexts.
